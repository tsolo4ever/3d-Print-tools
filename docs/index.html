Overview

The 3D Printer Calibration Suite is a collection of tools designed to help you calibrate and optimize your 3D printer for the best possible print quality.

All tools are web-based, require no installation, and work offline. Your data is stored locally on your device for privacy and convenience.

General Tips

Calibration Order

For best results, calibrate in this order:

  1. E-Steps - Foundation for all extrusion
  2. Flow Rate - Fine-tune extrusion amount
  3. Temperature - Optimize for your filament
  4. Retraction - Eliminate stringing

When to Recalibrate

  • After changing or upgrading extruder
  • When switching to a new filament brand/type
  • If you notice under or over-extrusion
  • Every 6-12 months for maintenance

Best Practices

  • Use fresh, dry filament for calibration
  • Perform tests at actual printing temperatures
  • Take multiple measurements and average
  • Document your settings for reference
  • Don't forget to save to EEPROM (M500)
